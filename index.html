Welcome to Move

THE RIDE OF YOUR LIFE

Move, a company established in 1992, is a luxury car dealer. We have brands from all over the world.

We started when our founder Alexander Move, a boy living on the outskirts of France took his passion for cars and wanted to share it with the world. He opened his first dealership in Paris, France since then Move has become a major conglomerate with 300 dealerships worldwide.

Alexander worked tirelessly to bring upon partnerships from various brands. To name a few: Mercedes Benz, Tesla, BMW, VW and Jeep. It is the first dealership of its kind.

We hope to see you in the future!
